I am an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto. I began my studies in Engineering Science (2023), but later transferred to the Faculty of Arts and Science (2024), where I now major in Mathematics and minor in Philosophy and Economics. My academic interests lie especially in Real Analysis, Logic, and the Philosophy of Mathematics.

The transfer was prompted by a deeper realization: I am most engaged not by building systems that simply work, but by understanding why they work. I am drawn to reasoning, structure, and explanation. I enjoy forming rigorous arguments for practical claims and value clear, coherent derivations over shortcuts. While I recognize the importance of practical outcomes, I believe they are best supported by careful, foundational thinking.

Outside of academics, I enjoy watching films and reading and writing about mathematics and philosophy. I also play chess, sudoku, calligraphy, and badminton.
