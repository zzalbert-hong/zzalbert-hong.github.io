I am a sophomore at the University of Toronto specializing in Mathematics and minoring in Philosophy and Economics. I write about mathematics and philosophy, and I am particularly interested in Real Analysis, Logic, and the Philosophy of Mathematics. (See my profile.)

I built this website for 3 purposes:

  1. To provide resources for future students in mathematics, etc.
  2. To give recruiters and collaborators a better sense of who I am
  3. To organize and preserve my work and thoughts
This website contains The Articles page, which contains my expositions or reflections on math and philosophy The Courses page, which contains notes and study guides from courses I have taken And finally, the Other page, which contains recommendations and my older projects.
