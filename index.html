About Me

I am a sophomore at the University of Toronto specializing in Mathematics and minoring in Philosophy and Economics. I am interested in Real Analysis, Logic, and Philosophy of Mathematics. See my profile.

About This Website

I built this website for 3 purposes:

  1. As a profile to help recruiters know me more
  2. To provide resources for future students in mathematics, etc.
  3. As a place to keep my works and thoughts
The Profile page contains information about my education, experiences, and certificates, etc.; it is intended to be like a resume. The Articles page contains essays I have written to explain mathematical concepts or express my philosophical views, etc.; it is intended to be like a blog. The Courses page contains notes and guides for courses I have taken or TA'ed before. The Research page contains descriptions about my research topics. Finally, the Other page contains recommendations or other projects I have done in the past.
