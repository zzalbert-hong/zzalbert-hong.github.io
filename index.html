I am a sophomore at the University of Toronto specializing in Mathematics and minoring in Philosophy and Economics. I write about mathematics and philosophy, and I am particularly interested in Real Analysis, Logic, and the Philosophy of Mathematics. (See my profile.)

I built this website for 3 purposes:

  1. to provide resources for future students, especially those in mathematics,
  2. to give recruiters and collaborators a better sense of who I am, and
  3. to organize and store my works and thoughts.
Content include my notes or study guides for courses at UofT, my exposition and reflection articles on mathematics and philosophy, external resources I recommend, etc. Below is the structure of the website:
