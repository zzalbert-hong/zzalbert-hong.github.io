Mathematics

Limits Part I (webpage; 2025.05.20): The most confusing topic that first year math students encounter is perhaps the Epsilon-Delta Definition for limits. For one, students struggle to understand the statement; for another, it is not clear why limits are defined like so. In this article, I explain and motivate the Definition from a special perspective.

Limits Part II (PDF; 2025.05.20): Another confusing aspect about limits is the idea of "approaching but not touching." A lot of students ask: Are limits actually equal to some value or infinitely approaching but not equal? In this article, I explain how to understand and use the terminology "limit."

Limits Part III (PDF; 2025.05.20): While we often associate limits with specific ideas, there are peculiar functions that challenge these inuitions. I have wrote this article to provide a few, and share my attitude towards those cases.
