I started this blog in Summer 2025, motivated by 3Blue1Brown's Summer of Math Expositions (SoME). Hopefully I continue writing, and hopefully somebody finds them helpful. Feel free to shoot me an email if there is something you want me to write about, or even just to let me know how you feel about my articles. I might not have the time to respond, but I do read all emails.

My target audience is mainly students transitioning from high school to university-level mathematics, but I aim to provide enough background so that learners at all levels can benefit.

Below are articles I have written recently, including mathematical or philosophical expositions and discussions. Or see here for the complete list, organized by topics.
